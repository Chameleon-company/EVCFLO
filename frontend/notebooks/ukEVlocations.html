Pre-requisites data manipulation steps in the "uk_location_processed.csv" file.¶

  1. Map AddressInfo.Town name with AddressInfo.Postcode according to the UK postcode list https://ideal-postcodes.co.uk/guides/postcode-areas
  2. Correct postcode format in some records.
  3. Update those records with capital letters in AddressInfo.Town to be smaller letters.
  4. Filter out records from the countries which are not part of the UK ie, Isle of Man, Ireland and Channel Islands.
  5. Filter out records with imcomplete coordinates.
